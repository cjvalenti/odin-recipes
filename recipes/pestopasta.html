Pesto Pasta

Delicious, Creamy & Fresh Pesto Pasta

This incredible pesto pasta gets its richness from pine nuts, parmesan cheese and high-quality olive oil.
Its bright, fresh notes come from fresh basil and a squeeze of lemon. Read on to learn how to make this delicious dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Pasta
  • 3 Cups Basil Leaves
  • 3/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Pine Nuts
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Teaspoons Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. In a large pot, boil salted water for pasta.
  2. While water boils, combine pine nuts, basil, parmesan, olive oil and garlic in a food processor.
  3. Once all ingredients are combined, season with salt, pepper and lemon to taste.
  4. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  5. Coat pasta with the finished pesto sauce. Enjoy!
